Know Us






Historically, numerous garage start-ups have gone big! Goaded by this statistic and with a small push from the family’s ladies, Juhi Mehta decided to put her culinary talents to use.

On 25th November 2015, armed with basic baking knowledge and some other decoration lessons, Juhi took over her family’s kitchen.

The first cake sponge rolled out and was decorated beautifully.

After that, there was no looking back. Order after order was received and completed. The reward, clients’ smiles and compliments (they did pay for the cake!).

Chocolates, cake pops, desserts, cookies, cupcakes and cake jars joined Creamy Confection’s arsenal.

Participations in the Lil-Flea and Melange, also gave an impetus to the business.

Today, it has been more than 2 years and we have lost count of the product roll out, but we still remember each of our clients and their feedbacks.

We still yearn to hear that sweetness in their compliments which is accentuated by our delectable confectionaries.

Come along, hop on to our journey, we’re still in the first gear.
